Hi there! In this first post I welcome everyone to my blog which is planned mainly to amalgamate all the different varieties of solutions I have been discovering in my University journey as a student. We will have note-taking solutions, book-servers, learning new software (as well as some of the oldest tech that still runs most of the world, behind the curtains), and even some of my general jot-downs. I hope my writing style will not discourage the readers to stop though, as I’m sure the content of these blogs will be indulging. I’d rather encourage you to read less of my blogs, take the keywords or ideas and do your own discoveries.

To begin, begin.

-William Wordsworth

Now, a bit about me. I myself am a big Linux/Unix enthusiast (although I dare not say I’m an expert), I like tinkering with stuff. One of my hobbies is electronics, however I have yet not been able to learn or do much in that field. As a coder, I am still very much a beginner and hope to learn more and more. These blogs will not only help me to avoid procrastination, but also help my readers to learn something new.

All of these posts are written on a weekend in my Raspberry Pi 3b+ (which I use as a daily driver even though it is not meant to be used that extensively). The name of my blog is not coincidental, as I find weekends to be the perfect time to get into new projects, books, or hobbies. I hope my readers will enjoy these projects and make their own ones that I can write about in these very blogs.
