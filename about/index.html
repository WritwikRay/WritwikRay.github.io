WeekendsWithRay is a personal blog where I share tips and tricks about Linux, learn LaTex with a little bit of Arduino scripting here and there. I am currently an Economics undergrad from the University of Calcutta.

The broad idea of the blog is to take charge of the weekends and try to have fun while being productive! I hope these small blogs are going to help people redefine weekend getaways and encourage them to learn as well as teach more and more about Linux, LaTex and many more things!

The blog is powered by Hugo. Learn more and contribute on Github. The theme is contributed by Wisnu G Saputra.
